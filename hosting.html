What's the difference between Metrolodges and other channel managers?

Other channel managers are built for hotels and cost ₹2,000-5,000/month per listing, they only sync calendars and are complex to use. Metrolodges is the first channel manager built exclusively for homestays. We're affordable (starting at Rs 490/- per listing) and do much more: channel management, property management, pricing optimization, direct bookings, guest verification, and support — all in one simple platform.

How does the 0% Commission + Channel Manager (Subscription) work?

  • With Metrolodges, you pay 0% commission on bookings and keep 100% of your earnings.
  • Guests can book directly with you via WhatsApp, but only after completing secure ID verification, ensuring safety and trust.
  • You get an in-built channel manager that automatically syncs your property details, calendars, and pricing across major OTAs like Metrolodges, Airbnb, MakeMyTrip, Agoda, Booking.com, and more.
  • This seamless sync prevents double bookings while maximizing your exposure and earnings.

How is the Channel Manager connected?

  • Once you list your property on Metrolodges, our team will reach out to help you sync your property data with all major OTAs like Airbnb, MakeMyTrip, Agoda, and more — this is a one-time setup.
  • We'll also provide you with a demo to guide you through managing your entire business from the Metrolodges app. After this, you won't need any other app or platform to manage bookings, calendars, pricing, or availability — everything happens seamlessly in one place.
